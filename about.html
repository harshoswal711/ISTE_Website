Why Us.

Major benefits of joining ISTE is that you get to be a part of an academic fraternity of strong professionals. You are eligible to participate in many national events. ISTE provides guidance and training to students to develop better learning skills and personality.

Our Mission.

We aim to enhance the leadership qualities among students and provide them a platform which enriches their social as well as interpersonal skills and acquaint them with extra classroom knowledge for their overall growth.

What we Do.

We organize technical workshops, seminars to provide insights to students. Give industrial visits, arrange intercollegiate competitions to embed teamwork skills. Training programs are devised to assist the young minds update their knowledge.
